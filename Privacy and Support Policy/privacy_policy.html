Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Country News" mobile application (the "App") handles your information.

Data Collection

  • No Personal Data: The App does not require user accounts, logins, or registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking pixels to monitor your behavior across other apps or websites.
  • RSS Feed Content: The App displays news content fetched via News RSS feeds from Google/Bing/BBC. Your interactions with the news headlines are local to your device.

Third-Party Services

When you view news headlines, the App fetches data from public available News Feeds provided by Google/Bing/BBC. Please note that clicking on a news headline will open the original news source in your device's web browser, where that site's own privacy policy will apply.

Data Storage

All app settings (such as country selection) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our support page.
